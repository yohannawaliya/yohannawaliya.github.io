Yohanna Joseph Waliya
Yohanna Joseph Waliya is a researcher, lecturer at the University of Calabar, Nigerian digital poet, distant writer, ludokinetic writer, novelist, playwright, python programmer, winner of the Janusz Korczak Prize for Global South 2020 , Electronic Literature Organization Research Fellow, UNESCO Janusz Korczak Fellow, Creator & Curator of MAELD & ADELD [2022 Emerging Open Scholarship Award: Honourable Mention, C-SKI], Executive Director of AELA& ADELI, International Association for Advancement of Artificial Intelligence Conference on Web and Social Media [ICWSM ] Virtual Scholar 2021-2022, and Hastac Scholar 2021-2023. He writes in English and French. Among his works are : La récolte de vie (Théâtre), Monde 2.0 (Théâtre), Hégémonie Disparue (Roman), Les Dieux et leurs Subalternes ont Tort (La Poésie philosophique),Quand l’Afrique se lèvera (Roman),Souffrance c'est une grandeur (Roman philosophique), Homosalus (digital poetry), Momenta (digital poetry),ZeroDeath(digital poetry)|ZéroDèces(poésie animée),Truthology(digital poetry) | Véritologie(poésie animée), @TinyKorczak(Twitterbot-poetry),@KorczakD(Twitterbot-playwright), Climatophosis (digital poetry: The best use of DH for Fun 2020), Inferno 2.0 (ludokinetic poetry), Cyberpoetry for activism(digital poetry)| Cyberpoésies d'engagement(poésie animée), Letter to the African Union(digital poetry)| Lettre à Union Africaine(poésie animée), Jesus is above Cresus and Zeus| Jésus est au-dessus de Crésus.He is also postgraduate student at Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria. His research interests cover distant writing, Distant reading, Digital Poetry, Metaversal literature, Twitterbot-poetry , Twitterature, Digital Humanities, language discourse etc.
